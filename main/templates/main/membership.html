Interested in a Membership?

Joining our Shady Oaks Golf Club memberships service has its perks. Enjoy discounts at our in-house restaurant, equipment rentals, and lessons. Use your membership to get prioritized tee times and beat the crowd, or gain exclusive VIP access to guest speakers and events.

Your membership can also be used to receive discounts at select, local restaurants and entertainment establishments in the metro area.

Contact us at (402)-235-3673 to speak to a friendly member of our staff, or come visit us at the course to get your membership started!
