Contact Shady Oaks

To contact a course instructor or representative, please use the information provided below. Send us a message, call us directly, or stop in. Shady Oaks would love to hear from you!


By Phone

You can contact Shady Oaks at the club house pro shop anytime from 9AM - 7PM Tuesday thru Sunday. We are closed for business on Mondays.

Phone: (402) 235-3673(FORE)


By Email

Emails are welcome at any time - and respond to emails in 1-2 business days. We respond to emails, that are sent out of business hours (9AM - 7PM Tuesday thru Sunday) on the following business day.

Email: info@shadyoaks.com


Visit Us at the Course:

You're always welcome to stop into the pro shop located at the club house and speak with one of our professionals.

Address: 3673 Oak Street, Omaha, Nebraska 68037
