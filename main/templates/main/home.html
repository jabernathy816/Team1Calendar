Welcome to Shady Oaks Golf Course!

Hello and welcome to Shady Oaks - your premier destination for individual and group golf outings, events, banquets and much more. Here at Shady Oaks we pride ourselves on a quality, well-groomed 18-hole golf course. We have friendly and helpful staff available to ensure you're provided with an excellent customer service experience. Shady Oaks can provide customers with dependable rentals and services that make your golf outing easier and more enjoyable. Use our links in the navigation tab above to schedule a tee time, obtain rentals of our premium inventory, or even check out our upcoming events to take part in the action!

Being a public course, anyone is welcome at Shady Oaks. We can make accommodations for any special needs that you may have. In 2018, Shady Oaks received recognition for being the best 18-hole golf course in Omaha, Nebraska. Follow us on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram for the latest on Shady Oaks special events and daily golfer shout outs!
